I am a Senior Researcher at GESIS - Leibniz Institute for the Social Sciences, where I manage the Comparative Study of Elections Surveys and conduct research on European politics, democratic governance, and party competition.

Previously I was a postdoctoral fellow at the University of Amsterdam and PhD researcher at the University of Glasgow.

My research examines how political actors and institutions shape public attitudes and what these dynamics mean for democratic governance in Europe.
